Project UNITY®

  • Project Unity® is a range of high performance water cooled PCs built for the ultimate enthusiast. Our expert technicians craft these systems by hand using the world's most powerful components from Intel, NVIDIA, AMD & ASUS.
  • All Project Unity® systems are designed & built with one mission in mind; to deliver maximum computing performance, operate cool and quiet, with stunning aesthetics.
  • Entry Kit
  • The 'Entry Kit' in our Unity® systems range offers superior performance over any all-in-one or air cooler. The kit is extremely silent to run and is aesthetically more appealing than a non custom loop system. The 'Entry Kit' is your first step. As with all our Unity® systems, you can customise your PC with our wide range of colours .
  • Prices from as little as:
  • £400
  • Mid Kit
  • The 'Mid Kit' in our Unity® systems range integrates the GPU into the loop for the superior cooling and overclock potential for your graphics card. The kit features a thicker radiator, larger reservoir and a more powerful pump to increase flow rate. As with all our Unity® systems, you can customise your PC with our wide range of colours.
  • Prices start from:
  • £700
  • High Kit
  • The "High Kit' in our Unity® systems range is the ultimate water-cooled system. The kit features two radiators to allow for optimal cooling potential. The 'Extreme Kit' is for the enthusiast gamer looking to experience the best of the best. As with all our Unity® systems, you can customise your PC with our wide range of colours.
